# TikTok Script Templates for a U.S. English-Speaking Female Audience

## 1. Target Audience Fit

Primary audience:

- English-speaking users in the U.S.
- Mostly women
- Best-fit age range: 20s to early 40s

Topics that usually resonate most:

- Love and relationships
- Self-worth and healing
- Overthinking and emotional clarity
- Career decisions and confidence
- Money mindset and timing

Tone to aim for:

- Warm
- Soft but direct
- A little mystical
- Practical enough to feel useful

Platform language to use:

- `link in bio`
- `comment "claim"`
- `for the girl who...`
- `if this found you...`
- `this is your sign`

## 2. Core Script Formula

Use this structure in almost every video:

1. Hook
2. Reading or result
3. What she should do next
4. Why that helps
5. CTA

Recommended video length:

- 10 to 15 seconds for quick daily energy videos
- 20 to 30 seconds for love, career, or money readings
- 30 to 45 seconds for fuller `result -> action -> value` videos

Swap-in variables:

- `[energy]`
- `[warning]`
- `[action]`
- `[benefit]`
- `[topic]`
- `[cta]`

## 3. Writing Style Rules

What works better for this audience:

- Speak like a friend, not like a formal horoscope writer
- Lead with emotion or tension in the first two seconds
- Give one simple action, not five tips
- Make the value personal: peace, clarity, confidence, protection, momentum
- Use `you` language constantly

What to avoid:

- Stiff translated phrasing
- Long spiritual explanations
- Too many zodiac terms in one video
- Generic lines with no action step
