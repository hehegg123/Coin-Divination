# AI Video Production Plan

## Goal

Create 3 launch videos for the website:

1. A homepage hero video
2. A short explainer for `Coin Divination` and `Bazi`
3. A website tutorial video

Audience:

- U.S. English-speaking users
- Mostly women
- Interested in love, clarity, timing, healing, and guidance

Recommended style:

- Soft mystical
- Clean and modern
- Emotionally warm
- Practical, not too abstract

## Recommended AI Stack

### Video generation

- `Runway` or `Sora` for atmospheric intro shots
- Use these for coins, candlelight, chart textures, symbolic transitions, and mood footage

### AI presenter / voice

- `HeyGen` or `Synthesia` for the explainer video
- Best for a calm female presenter speaking directly to camera

### Screen recording and editing

- `Descript` or `Captions`
- Best for the product tutorial video
- Use them for screen recording, AI voice cleanup, captions, pauses, and simple edits

### Design support

- `Canva`, `Figma`, or simple image prompts for title cards and overlays

## Production Strategy

Do not make all 3 videos the same way.

### Video 1: Homepage hero video

- Primary job: attract and orient new visitors fast
- Best format: atmospheric montage
- Use AI-generated visuals plus a tiny bit of real UI

### Video 2: What is Coin Divination and Bazi?

- Primary job: build trust and explain the method
- Best format: AI presenter + concept visuals

### Video 3: How to use the website

- Primary job: remove friction
- Best format: real screen recording + AI voice + captions

## Video 2: What Is Coin Divination and Bazi?

### Goal

Explain the system simply and make users trust the method.

### Length

- 35 to 50 seconds

### Format

- AI presenter on screen
- cutaways to coins, lines, calendar, chart texture, website UI

### Recommended structure

1. What is Coin Divination?
2. What is Bazi?
3. Why combine both?
4. What do you get on this site?

### English script

`If you’re new here, here’s what this reading actually is.`

`Coin Divination is a traditional method of asking for guidance through a six-line coin cast. It helps reveal the energy around your question right now.`

`Bazi, also called the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a birth chart system based on your date and time of birth. It shows the deeper pattern behind your personality, timing, strengths, and life tendencies.`

`When we combine them, Bazi gives the background, and the coin cast gives the message of the moment.`

`So instead of a generic horoscope, you get a reading that connects your chart with today’s timing.`

`On this site, you’ll see a quick reading first, then a deeper breakdown if you want more detail.`

`If you want to try your own reading, start in the link on the page.`

## Video 3: How To Use the Website

### Goal

Show exactly how to use the product in under 1 minute.

### Length

- 45 to 75 seconds

### Format

- real screen recording
- AI cleaned voiceover
- captions
- zooms and highlights

### What to show from the current site

Use these actual steps:

1. Choose language
2. Enter birth date
3. Enter birth time
4. Select gender
5. Choose focus area
6. Pick `Quick Reading` or `Traditional Coin Casting`
7. Generate the reading
8. Review the summary
9. Open the deeper breakdown

### English tutorial script

`Here’s how to use the reading in under a minute.`

`First, choose your language at the top.`

`Then enter your birth date and your birth time. If you don’t know the exact time, use the closest one you remember for now.`

`Next, choose your focus for today, like love, wealth, career, health, or an overall reading.`

`After that, choose how you want to cast.`

`If you want a faster experience, use Quick Reading and let the site cast for you.`

`If you want a more traditional experience, choose Traditional Coin Casting and enter all six coin lines yourself.`

`Then click Generate Today’s Reading.`

`You’ll see your main reading first, including the overall message, suggested action, and what the reading means for today.`

`If you want more detail, open the full chart breakdown to see the hexagram and the Bazi-based reading together.`

`That’s it. A quick reading first, and deeper insight if you want it.`

### Recording checklist

- Record on desktop first
- Use one clean sample birth date and birth time
- Show both `Quick Reading` and `Traditional Coin Casting`
- Use a sample result already loaded once if needed for a smoother second take
- Keep cursor movement slow and intentional

### Editing notes

- Add captions throughout
- Add soft zoom-ins on each step
- Add step labels like `Step 1`, `Step 2`, `Step 3`
- Highlight `Suggested Action` and `See the full chart breakdown`

Assemble:

- Video 1 from AI visuals + UI flashes

## Phase 5: Polish

Add:

- captions
- consistent fonts
- warm sound bed
- subtle transitions
- final CTA

## Creative Rules

- Keep all language simple and modern
- Avoid sounding too occult or too academic
- Always connect the idea to daily clarity and action
- Make the visuals feel premium, not spooky-cheap
- Speak to women like a trusted guide, not like a theatrical psychic
